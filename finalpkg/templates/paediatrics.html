Department of Paediatrics

The diversity of developmental age groups of children and the scope of services we provide to promote health, prevent disease/injuries, and cure diseases/injuries in children define the uniqueness of our specialty. Children differ from adults anatomically, physiologically, immunologically, developmentally, and metabolically. Helping them reach their full potential remains our department's ultimate goal. Our specialties include:

  • Cardiology
  • Adolescent Endocrinology
  • Oncology
  • Infectious Diseases
  • Dermatology
  • Emergency Paediatrics
  • Nutrition
  • Neurology
  • Respiratory
  • Nephrology
