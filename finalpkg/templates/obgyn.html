Department of Obstetrics and Gyneacology

The hospital's Obstetrics and Gynecology department is a busy department that ensures proper patient care and safe delivery by using evidence-based practices in a safe and comfortable working environment. The department's main primary processes include research, training and services. The department is divided into several sub-units, including:

  • Oncology and Pathological Unit
  • Reproductive Endocrinology and Fertility Regulation
  • Ultrasound and Fetal Medicine Unit
  • Adolescent Gyneacological Disorders Unit
