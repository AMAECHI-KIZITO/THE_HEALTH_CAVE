Department of Physiotherapy

The Pysiotherary department is involved in the day to day management and treatment of both in and outpatients who require physical therapy. We manage patients from the following areas of specialty:

  • Women health
  • Child health paediatrics
  • Neurology
  • Trauma/Orthopaedic
  • Emergency
  • Surgical (Pre and Post)
  • General rehabilitation
